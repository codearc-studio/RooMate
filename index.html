Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

RooMate connects directly to school data sources like Google Sheets for athletics and calendar feeds for events, presenting everything in a clean, fast interface.

Class Schedule

Track your full class schedule with custom setup per school level. Personalize your classes and get a clean daily view of what's next.

Custom Setup Daily View Personalized

Dashboard

Your central home view — combines schedule, sports, and events at a glance. See everything happening today without switching apps.

Today View Unified

Sports Hub

Live integration with AFS athletics via Google Sheets. Upcoming games, team schedules, home/away indicators, and coach notes — all updated automatically.

School Events

About

Built for AFS,
by a student.

RooMate started as a personal project to solve a real problem: too many places to check, not enough time between classes. It's a private project, not open source — built with SwiftUI and real AFS data sources.

The goal is simple: one fast, clean place for everything that matters in your school day.
